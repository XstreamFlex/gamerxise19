Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder who, as an ADHD and TBI survivor, struggled with chronic migraines and maintaining healthy exercise habits. He realized that the immersive nature of video games could be leveraged to create a unique fitness solution. Through trial and error, GamerXise was born?a program that merges video games and exercise to improve both physical and mental health. Our mission is to share this innovative approach with the world, helping gamers achieve their health and fitness goals while enjoying their favorite pastime.

Our Services

GamerXise Monthly

A monthly subscription providing new workouts every month. Get guided workouts and resources to make exercising while gaming easy. Includes educational resources and bonuses to enhance your results.

GamerXise Client

For those with specific goals or needing a tailored program. Receive personalized training, assessments, and program adjustments from a dedicated personal trainer. Ideal for improving game skills, overcoming health challenges, or achieving specific physical targets.

GamerXise Programming

Get a semi-personalized program designed by a personal trainer. Receive monthly updates and adjustments to your program, ensuring it remains effective and aligned with your goals.
